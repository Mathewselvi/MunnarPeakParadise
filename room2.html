Premium Deluxe Room

Room Overview

Discover the perfect blend of comfort and elegance in our Deluxe Mountain Room, offering magnificent views of Munnar's renowned mountain ranges. This thoughtfully designed accommodation provides intimate luxury for couples or small families seeking a peaceful retreat.

Wake up to the enchanting sight of emerald mountains cascading down the hillsides. The room features premium furnishings, modern amenities, and a private balcony where you can savor your morning coffee while immersed in nature's tranquility.

Experience authentic tea estate living with easy access to plantation walks, tea tasting sessions, and the serene beauty that makes Munnar a truly magical destination.
