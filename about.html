Welcome to Munnar Peak Paradise

Nestled in the heart of Kerala's stunning Western Ghats, Munnar Peak Paradise stands as a beacon of luxury and tranquility amidst one of India's most beautiful hill stations. Our resort offers an unparalleled experience where modern comfort meets the pristine beauty of nature.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing guests with an exceptional stay that combines world-class hospitality with the serene charm of Munnar's tea gardens, rolling hills, and cool mountain air.

Every aspect of our resort has been carefully designed to showcase the natural beauty of Munnar while providing our guests with the finest amenities and personalized service that makes each stay memorable.

Our Location in Munnar

Perfect Mountain Setting

Munnar Peak Paradise is ideally situated in the heart of Munnar, offering breathtaking views and easy access to the region's most spectacular attractions. Our location provides the perfect balance of serenity and accessibility.

Located at an altitude of 1600 meters above sea level, our resort enjoys a pleasant climate year-round, making it the perfect escape from the heat and humidity of the plains.
